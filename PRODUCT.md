# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Barn Jukebox serves guests at one private barn party and the host managing the night from a laptop. Guests are usually on phones, often standing around with music already playing, and need fast name entry, song search, and queue visibility. The host needs a reliable control surface for ordering requests and starting music playback without explaining the system.

## Product Purpose

Barn Jukebox creates one shared party queue for song requests. Success means guests can request songs without accounts, everyone sees the same live queue, and the host can move through the night confidently using Spotify for music playback and lyrics.

## Brand Personality

Fun, direct, and legible. The app should feel like a polished barn-party tool: lively enough for a social event, restrained enough to trust on a laptop, and readable on phones in dim evening light.

## Anti-references

Avoid cheesy western decoration, busy party graphics, marketing-page composition, tiny controls, or anything that makes the host wonder which list is authoritative.

## Design Principles

- One queue is the source of truth.
- The next action should be obvious to the host.
- Guests should always know where they are in line.
- Favor large, readable controls over decorative flourish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast dark-mode surfaces, large touch targets, visible focus states, and straightforward labels. Keep motion minimal and nonessential. The app should remain usable on phones in low light and on a laptop at party distance.
